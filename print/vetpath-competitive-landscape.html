Personalized plan key: ✔ yes · ◐ partial / generic · ✘ no  |  Most tools are a benefit matcher, a resource directory, or paid claims help — few deliver a free, prioritized, whole-life plan.
VetPath's wedge: the only free tool that turns a veteran's goals + life stage into a prioritized 30/60/90 gameplan across all domains, with in-person facilitation at meetings/VSOs — and a hard rule to verify with official sources and never charge for claims.
