HOW TO OPEN THE VETPATH APP (takes 10 seconds)

1. Save the file "VetPath-Interactive-App.html" to your computer
   (in your email: click the attachment, choose Download - do NOT just preview it;
   the preview window shows a blank or static page).

2. Open your Downloads folder.

3. Double-click "VetPath-Interactive-App.html".
   It opens in your web browser (Edge/Chrome) and works completely.

TIPS ONCE IT'S OPEN
- Click "Strategy" in the top menu, then "Load a sample veteran & plan" to see a filled example.
- Click "Pathfinder" then "Help me decide" and answer the 9 questions as yourself.
- The "Aa" button in the top bar makes all text bigger.

Your answers stay on your computer only - nothing is sent anywhere.
This is a planning & education prototype - not the VA. Benefit info shown is sample data;
always verify at VA.gov or with a free accredited VSO.
